About Us

The mission of The Byte Club is to inspire readers in making a positive impact in their lives and on those around them. Through its content, the magazine encourages and enables people to improve their knowledge, skills and engage in dialogue wherever they are: in college or office, in their families, neighborhoods and faith communities.
